Bangalore (officially Bengaluru) is the capital of Karnataka, India, 
famous as the nation's "Silicon Valley" due to its booming IT sector, 
but also known as the "Garden City" for its parks, diverse culture, 
pleasant climate, and rich history. It's a major metropolis on the Deccan Plateau, known for innovation, education, a thriving arts scene, and being a hub for technology, 
startups, and diverse cuisine.
